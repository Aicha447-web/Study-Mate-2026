# Study Mate – Study Guide

How to use Study Mate to study better and work with others.

---

## Getting Started

1. **Sign up** with your email and password.
2. **Log in** and go to your Dashboard.
3. **Add your courses** (Courses → Add Course) so you can find matching groups.

---

## Using Study Mate for Studying

### Step 1: Add Your Courses
- Go to **Courses**.
- Click **Add Course**.
- Enter course code (e.g. CS101) and name.
- Use **Search** to find courses by code or name.

### Step 2: Find or Create a Group
- Go to **Find Groups**.
- Choose a **course** and **goal** (exam prep, project, homework, review).
- Click **Find** to see groups you can join.
- Or create your own: **Groups** → **Create Group** → fill in name, course, goal, max members (2–5), description.

### Step 3: Work With Your Group
- Open **Groups** to see your groups.
- Click a group to see **members** and **Rate** peers after you work together.
- Use **Places** to add study locations (library, café, etc.) and share with others.

### Step 4: Use the AI Assistant
- Click the **AI icon** in the header.
- Ask for **study tips**, **time management**, or **group advice**.
- Say **"Create groups for CS101"** (or your course) to get help forming study groups.

---

## Study Tips

- **Add all your courses** so matching works well.
- **Rate peers** after group work so others can see who is reliable.
- **Use Find Groups** to join groups that match your course and goal.
- **Set a clear goal** when creating a group (exam prep, project, etc.).

---

| What you want | Where to go |
